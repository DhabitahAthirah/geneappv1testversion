Analysis Tools:

Species Identification Search

By making use of the BLAST API, enter a gene sequence in a form of FASTA and compare it with that of an already existing gene database such as NCBI.

Restriction Enzyme Mapping

Maps sites for restriction enzymes, a.k.a. restriction endonucleases, by entering a gene sequence.

Gene Graph Tool

Enter a gene sequence and map the amino acids in graph format.
